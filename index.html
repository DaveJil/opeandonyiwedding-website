Traditional Marriage 11AM - 5PM

It's a colorful gathering where families come together to witness the couple's commitment to each other. The ceremony involves various essential elements, including the payment of bride price (often in the form of kola nuts, wine, and other symbolic items), cultural dances, and the exchange of vows, followed by a grand feast. Ogba traditional marriages are a blend of tradition, spirituality, and festivity, reflecting the cultural richness and unity of the Ogba people.

9th December

Church Service 11AM-12PM

The church wedding is a sacred and joyful celebration that unites two hearts in the presence of God and their loved ones. It typically takes place in a church, where the couple seeks blessings for their union. The ceremony includes prayers, scripture readings, hymns, and a solemn exchange of vows. Dressed in exquisite attire, the bride and groom radiate elegance and charm. Family and friends come together to witness this momentous occasion, offering their support and love to the newlyweds. Following the ceremony, a vibrant reception often ensues, featuring music, dancing, and a sumptuous feast, making the church wedding a beautiful blend of faith, tradition, and jubilation.

Opeyemi and Beauty: A Journey to Forever

In the heart of Obafemi Awolowo University, two souls began a love story destined to last a lifetime. Opeyemi and Beauty's enchanting tale of love unfolded in the most serendipitous way.

Chapter One: A Fateful Encounter

In 2015, their paths crossed for the first time. Opeyemi left a campus restaurant, and Beauty, radiant as the sun, approached it. Fate brought them inches apart but withheld their introduction.A year later, in September 2016, Opeyemi, now a year ahead, took a leap of faith. He approached Beauty and her friend Ife, marking their first official interaction.

Chapter Two: A Journey of Love

Their love story unfolded through enchanting dates and adventures. Their first date in September 2016 set the stage for countless more. In November 2016, they ventured to Ibadan, celebrating their love's two-month mark. Distance briefly separated them after Opeyemi's graduation in May 2017, but their love remained unshaken.

Chapter Three: Family Bonds

December 2017 was a turning point. Beauty made her grand debut to Opeyemi's family at their Christmas party, a pivotal moment as Opeyemi introduced Beauty as his girlfriend. Throughout 2018, Beauty's dedication bridged the distance between school and Lagos. Their love grew stronger.

Chapter Four: A New Beginning

In late 2018, Beauty's move to Lagos brought them closer. They explored the vibrant streets of Lagos, visiting the renowned Nike Art Gallery and enjoying serene beach houses. Boat rides brought them even closer.

Chapter Five: Love Takes Flight

July 2019 was the turning point. Beauty fully moved to Lagos, and they embarked on a grand adventure – a 10-day journey to Dubai, UAE, accompanied by their dear friend Abiola. From desert safaris to helicopter rides, they embraced love's boundless possibilities.

Chapter Six: A Proposal of a Lifetime

Their story took an even more heartfelt turn when Opeyemi organized a surprise family picnic dinner on Beauty's birthday in 2023. Under a canopy of stars, Opeyemi proposed to Beauty, sealing their destiny with tears of joy and radiant smiles. Friends, family, and cherished ones surrounded them, making it an enchanting declaration of love.

Chapter Seven: A New Beginning

On the 3rd of March 2023, Opeyemi and Beauty officially bound their love in a small and intimate civil wedding. Surrounded by close family members and cherished siblings, they made their love official, marking the start of their journey as a married couple. Their apartment's poolside in Oniru was the venue for a joyous reception, with both sets of parents and beloved siblings uniting in love and shared joy.

Their love story continued to unfold, a narrative of enduring love and the promise of an everlasting bond. Opeyemi and Beauty's journey had now transitioned from the pages of affectionate courtship to the beautifully illuminated manuscript of a married life, with countless chapters of love and togetherness waiting to be written.
